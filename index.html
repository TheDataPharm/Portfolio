PHARMACOVIGILANCE / RWE

The Contraception Safety Paradox

Do "natural" wellness trends align with clinical reality? I analyzed 12,425 FDA adverse event reports to compare the safety profiles of the Copper IUD vs. Hormonal Pills. The findings revealed a 6.5x higher clot risk for pills, but a surprising 2.5x higher depression signal for the non-hormonal IUD.

Python (Pandas) • Power BI • Pharmacovigilance

GLP-1 Safety Signal Dashboard

Analyzed 200,000+ FDA adverse event reports to distinguish between drug-induced risks and patient comorbidities. My pipeline identified a 2.3x higher hospitalization rate in diabetic patients vs. obesity patients, validated via Reporting Odds Ratio (ROR=0.92).

HEOR / BIOSTATISTICS

Oncology Financial Toxicity Analysis

Designed a cross-sectional study (n=73) to assess catastrophic healthcare expenditure among breast cancer patients at LUTH. Analyzed income stratification and direct medical costs using Kruskal-Wallis tests, revealing significant gaps in health insurance coverage (p=0.433).
